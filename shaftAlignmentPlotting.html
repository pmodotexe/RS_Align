Dimension Guide

How to Use

1. Setup Dimensions

Measure and enter the distances between your machines according to the diagram. You can switch between inches and millimeters using the unit selector in the header.

2. Take Dial Readings

Use your dial indicators to measure TIR values on both stationary and movable machines. Values can be entered in mils or micrometers.

3. Review Results

Follow the recommended adjustments to achieve proper shaft alignment. Results are shown in the same units you selected.
